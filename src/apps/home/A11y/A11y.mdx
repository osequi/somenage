### A11y

Enables inclusive design. For people with disabilities who browse the site with a screen reader. Or for power users who prefer keyboard navigation vs. mouse / touchpad.

#### Features

- Provides mouse, touch, keyboard, and screen reader interactions that have been tested across a wide variety of browsers, devices, and platforms.
- Supports stateful components.

#### Menu

- No interactive content in menu items (`<a>`): https://github.com/adobe/react-spectrum/issues/1244
-

#### What's done

- The app is wrapped into an `SSRProvider`: https://react-spectrum.adobe.com/react-aria/ssr.html.
- `useId` keeps stuff in sync with `SSRProvider`.
